GST Reconciliation, Reimagined

From 2 working days to 6 hours per filing cycle.

The context

At PrintStop, the finance team's monthly GST reconciliation was manual, spreadsheet-heavy, and error-prone, creating delays during filing cycles. Monthly invoice volumes ranged from 500 to 1,000.

My role

Improve speed and accuracy of reconciliation while creating a scalable, repeatable workflow the finance team could own.

The approach

  1. 1Mapped the end-to-end workflow with finance and operations to surface bottlenecks and define success metrics.
  2. 2Sequenced the solution into data standardization, matching logic design, and Power Query automation.
  3. 3Built a structured dump-sheet so monthly invoices auto-reconcile when fed in.
  4. 4Added validation checks, exception flags, documentation, and stakeholder feedback loops to govern the process.

The outcome

Reconciliation time dropped from ~2 working days to ~6 hours, accuracy improved, and the finance team shifted focus to higher-value analytical work.

OperationsGoogle Apps ScriptRisk & Controls

Job Process Dashboard, Trusted by the Factory

Rebuilt for reliability, adopted into daily station-level planning.

The context

A Google Apps Script automation streamlined a factory operations workflow built on a highly complex source spreadsheet. Because the factory team relied on it for job planning, accuracy was non-negotiable.

My role

Make sure the automation produced fully reliable outputs the factory team could trust for station-level planning.

The approach

  1. 1Spotted discrepancies between automated output and edge cases raised by stakeholders during initial rollout, and paused immediately.
  2. 2Ran a logic review that surfaced ambiguity in how certain business rules had been interpreted from the source data.
  3. 3Implemented structured input validation, exception flags for outliers, and a parallel-run mechanism comparing automation output with manual samples.
  4. 4Standardized requirement checkpoints with users to eliminate interpretation gaps and documented the logic clearly for future use.

The outcome

Strengthened controls eliminated discrepancies in subsequent runs, restored stakeholder confidence, and the automation was successfully adopted into the factory workflow.
