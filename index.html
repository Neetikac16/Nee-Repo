Case Study 01

GST 2B Reconciliation Automation

Rebuilt a manual, spreadsheet-heavy monthly reconciliation process into a scalable Power Query workflow for 500-1,000 invoices per cycle.

The challenge

The finance team's GST reconciliation was slow, error-prone, and highly manual, creating friction during filing cycles.

My role

Improve speed and accuracy while building a repeatable workflow the team could reliably use month after month.

Approach

Mapped the end-to-end process, defined success metrics, standardized data, designed matching logic, automated the workflow in Power Query, and embedded validation checks, exception flags, documentation, and stakeholder feedback loops.

Outcome

Reduced reconciliation time from roughly 2 working days to about 6 hours, improved data accuracy, and freed the finance team for higher-value analytical work.

Case Study 02

Factory Planner Dashboard

Built a Google Apps Script automation for factory job planning, then strengthened it through controls and parallel validation to ensure adoption-critical accuracy.

The challenge

The workflow relied on a highly complex source spreadsheet, and even minor logic errors could disrupt station-level factory planning.

My role

Deliver a tool the factory team could fully trust, with output reliability as the core success condition.

Approach

Paused rollout when edge cases surfaced, reviewed the logic, identified business rule ambiguity, then added structured input validation, exception flags, manual sample comparisons, clearer requirement checkpoints, and documentation.

Outcome

Eliminated discrepancies in subsequent runs, restored stakeholder confidence, and enabled successful adoption into the factory workflow.
